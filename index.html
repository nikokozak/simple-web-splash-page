simple web is feature packed.

easy to get started with,
but highly extensible.

docx xlsx pptx
md txt html
css jpg png

filetype support

we support anything libreoffice can convert and then some: everything from docx to md files.

blog pages

add a date onto your filename and you automatically get a blog, complete with an RSS feed.

full control

throw in a .css or .html file into the mix and fully control your site. Write an ERB template to pipe into the site generation.

photo pages

make beautiful carousels and gallery pages by just adding pictures to a folder.

email subscriptions

sign people up to your newsletter or blog by simply adding a special subscribe tag to one of your documents.

a shop in 5 seconds

create a shop by adding .txt files and .png files to a shop folder - link your shop up with paypal, stripe, and shopify.
